Dimension

Combine blinds, sheers and curtains to create layered window treatments that give you complete control over light, privacy and style.

Design Your Layers How It Works
The Three Layers

Building the Perfect Window

01 🪟

Layer 1: The Blind

Your functional foundation. Roller, venetian or honeycomb blinds sit closest to the glass, providing UV protection and precise light control.

02 🌤️

Layer 2: The Sheer

The middle layer diffuses natural light beautifully, softening the view while maintaining daytime privacy. Creates an ethereal glow.

03 🏡

Layer 3: The Curtain

The statement piece. S-fold or pinch pleat curtains frame your window, add insulation and complete the layered look with dramatic flair.

Popular Combinations

Curated Layer Pairings

The Classic Luxe

Blockout roller blind + sheer curtain + S-fold drape. Maximum versatility — go from full darkness to soft filtered light to wide open.

The Soft Living

Why Layer

The Benefits of Layered Treatments

🌗

Total Light Control

From blackout to filtered sunshine — adjust each layer independently for the perfect ambiance.

🔇

Acoustic Insulation

Multiple fabric layers absorb sound, creating a quieter, more peaceful space.

🌡️

Climate Control

Trapped air pockets between layers provide excellent thermal insulation year-round.

✨

Visual Depth

Layers create a sense of luxury and sophistication that single treatments simply can't match.
