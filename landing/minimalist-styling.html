Philosophy

Less, but better

Minimalist window styling isn't about having nothing — it's about having exactly the right thing. Every fold, every texture, every line serves a purpose.

We curate window treatments that integrate seamlessly into your architecture. No visual clutter. No unnecessary hardware. Just clean, intentional design that enhances the light and space you already have.

Curated Selection

Three Essentials

Every minimalist home needs at most three types of window treatment. We help you choose the right ones.

01

Roller Blinds

The purest expression of functional window covering. A single plane of fabric, disappearing completely when not needed.

  • Cassette headbox for invisible mounting
  • Slim 40mm roll diameter
  • Edge-to-edge fabric coverage
  • Matte, linen-look textures
  • Silent spring or motorised
02

Sheer Curtains

Diffused light, gentle movement, architectural softness. Sheers add warmth without weight to any space.

  • S-fold or ripplefold tracks
  • Recessed ceiling track option
  • Natural linen and cotton blends
  • Floor-to-ceiling draping
  • Weighted hems for clean lines
03

Panel Glides

For wide openings and sliding doors. Flat panels that stack neatly and move with architectural precision.

  • Clean vertical lines
  • Variable panel widths
  • Flush ceiling-mount track
  • Coordinate with roller fabrics
  • Stackable to one side
Design Principles

The Art of Simplicity

Four principles that guide every minimalist window treatment we design.

Hide the Hardware

Recessed tracks, cassette headboxes, and concealed brackets. The mechanism should be invisible — only the fabric remains.

Respect the Architecture

Window treatments should follow the room's geometry. Floor-to-ceiling installations extend the vertical line. Wall-to-wall coverage emphasises width.

Limit the Palette

One or two neutral tones across the entire home. Consistency creates calm. Variety creates visual noise.

Embrace Natural Light

Don't fight the light — shape it. Sheers diffuse, sunscreens filter, blockouts frame. Layer for control without complexity.
