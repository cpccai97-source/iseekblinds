We Understand

Apartment Living
Comes with Challenges

We've fitted thousands of Melbourne apartments. We know the common problems — and we have the solutions.

  • 🌆
    Overlooking Neighbours

    High-rise living means windows facing other buildings. Our dual roller blinds give you privacy without blocking natural light.

  • ☀️
    Harsh Western Sun

    West-facing apartments cop intense afternoon heat. Sunscreen roller blinds reduce heat by up to 80% while maintaining your view.

  • 📏
    Tight Window Reveals

    Shallow window reveals and limited mounting space? We specialise in face-fix and ceiling-mount solutions for tricky installs.

  • 🏗️
    Body Corp Restrictions

    Many buildings require uniform external appearance. We supply body-corp-approved white-backed blinds that meet building regulations.

  • 🔑
    Rental Properties

    Renters need non-damaging solutions. Our tension-fit and adhesive-mount blinds install without drilling — and come with you when you move.
