Bay Window Configurations We Cover

Every bay window is unique. We custom-fit solutions for all angles and styles.

📐

Angled Bay Windows

The classic three-panel bay with 30° or 45° angles. We precision-measure each panel for roller blinds, venetians or curtains that sit flush against each face.

🌀

Bow Windows

Curved bow windows with 4-6 panels need flexible tracking systems. Our curved curtain tracks and individual panel blinds create a seamless look.

🔲

Box Bay Windows

Square box bays with 90° angles are perfect for plantation shutters or roman blinds. Each side treated independently for maximum light control.

Our Bay Window Solutions

01

Plantation Shutters

The premium choice for bay windows. Custom-built panels follow your bay's exact angles, providing timeless elegance and full light control. Hinged or bi-fold options available.

02

Individual Roller Blinds

Each bay panel gets its own precisely fitted roller blind. Choose from blockout, sunscreen or translucent fabrics. Motorised options mean no dangling cords in tight spaces.

03

Curved Curtain Tracks

Bespoke curved tracks follow the contour of your bay perfectly. Pair with pinch-pleat, S-fold or ripplefold curtains for a flowing, unified look across the entire bay.

04

Venetian & Honeycomb Blinds

Individual venetian or honeycomb blinds per panel offer precise light and privacy control. Honeycomb blinds add excellent insulation—ideal for bay windows that catch cold draughts.

Bay Window FAQs

What's the best blind for a bay window?
It depends on your style and priorities. Plantation shutters are the premium choice for a classic look. Roller blinds offer a modern, clean line. Curtains on curved tracks create softness. We'll help you choose during our free consultation.
Can you do motorised blinds in a bay window?
Absolutely! Motorised roller or honeycomb blinds are a popular bay window choice. Each panel operates independently via remote or smart home integration. No cords means a cleaner look and improved child safety.
How do you handle the angles?
We laser-measure each panel individually, including the exact angles between panels. Each blind or shutter panel is manufactured to precise specifications. For curtains, we use custom-bent tracks that follow your bay's exact curve.
Are bay window blinds more expensive?
Bay windows typically require 3-5 individual panels, so the total cost is proportional. However, we offer bay window package pricing that's more competitive than pricing each panel separately. Contact us for a free quote.
Do you service heritage bay windows?
Yes! Many Melbourne heritage homes have stunning bay windows. We offer sympathetic solutions—timber shutters, traditional curtains on concealed tracks—that respect the character while adding modern functionality.
