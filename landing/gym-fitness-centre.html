FAQ

Common Questions

What blinds are best for a commercial gym?

Solar screen roller blinds with 5% openness are the industry standard — they block 95% of UV and reduce glare while maintaining outward visibility. For spin rooms and yoga spaces that need total darkness, dual roller systems with blockout backing are ideal. We always recommend commercial-grade motors for venues with large windows.

Can gym blinds handle moisture and sweat?

Absolutely. Our commercial range uses PVC-free moisture-resistant fabrics rated for wet-zone installation. They're mould-proof, antibacterial, and can be wiped clean with standard gym sanitiser. For pool areas, we use chlorine-resistant materials with marine-grade hardware.

How durable are commercial blinds compared to residential?

Commercial systems use heavier brackets, reinforced chains, and motors rated for 50,000+ cycles (vs. 10,000 residential). Fabrics are selected for UV stability, tear resistance, and cleanability. Our commercial installations come with a 5-year warranty including parts and labour.

Can blinds be scheduled to open and close automatically?

Yes. Our smart motor systems support scheduled operation via app, wall switch, or building management integration. Program blinds to open at 5am and close at 10pm automatically — perfect for 24/7 gyms with minimal staff. Group control allows up to 50 blinds to operate simultaneously.

Do you install during off-hours to minimise disruption?

We offer before-hours (5–8am) and after-hours (8pm–midnight) installation for operating gyms. Most single-location installs are completed in one session. We coordinate with your class schedule to ensure zero disruption to members.
