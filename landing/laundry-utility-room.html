Expert Tips for Laundry Blinds

Advice from our installers who've fitted thousands of wet-area blinds across Melbourne.

💨

Prioritise Ventilation

Choose louvred or slatted blinds that let air circulate. Good airflow reduces condensation on windows and walls, preventing long-term mould issues.

🚫

Avoid Fabric Blinds

Standard fabric roman blinds and curtains absorb moisture and become breeding grounds for mould. Stick to aluminium, PVC, or coated polyester.

🧽

Wipe Down Monthly

Even moisture-resistant blinds benefit from a monthly wipe with warm soapy water. This prevents soap residue and lint buildup from the washing cycle.

📏

Inside Mount Where Possible

Inside-mount blinds sit within the window recess, keeping them away from the tub and washing machine. This reduces splash exposure and keeps the look neat.

🎨

Go Light on Colour

White or light-coloured slats show less water spotting and lint dust. They also reflect more light into what's usually a small room.

🔒

Check Safety Compliance

If your laundry doubles as a kids' play area or connects to outdoor space, choose cordless or wand-operated blinds for child safety compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about blinds for laundry and utility rooms.

What's the best blind for a steamy laundry?

Aluminium venetian blinds are the number-one choice. The metal slats are completely impervious to steam and moisture, they let air circulate between slats when tilted open, and they're easy to wipe down. PVC verticals are a close second for larger windows.

Can I use timber venetians in my laundry?

We don't recommend real timber in wet areas — wood absorbs moisture, swells, and can warp over time. If you want the timber look, we offer PVC faux-wood venetians that look identical but handle moisture without any issues.

How do I clean laundry blinds?

Aluminium and PVC blinds just need a wipe with warm soapy water and a soft cloth. For roller blinds, spot-clean with a damp cloth. We recommend a thorough clean once a month to prevent detergent residue and lint buildup.

Will my blinds go mouldy in a humid laundry?

Not with our moisture-resistant range. Aluminium and PVC materials don't support mould growth. Our polyester roller fabrics are treated with anti-microbial coatings. The key is good ventilation — keep your blinds tilted open when not in use to allow air circulation.

Are laundry blinds child-safe?

All our products comply with Australian child safety standards. We offer cordless venetians, wand-operated verticals, spring-assisted rollers, and PVC shutters with no cords at all. If your laundry connects to play areas, we'll recommend the safest option for your setup.

How much do laundry room blinds cost?

Aluminium venetians start from around $95 per window, PVC verticals from $120, moisture-proof rollers from $140, and PVC shutters from $350. Final pricing depends on window size and product choice — we'll give you an exact quote during the free measure visit.

Can you install blinds above a laundry tub?

Absolutely. We use stainless steel brackets and mount blinds inside the window recess where possible to keep them above the splash zone. For windows directly above tubs, we recommend aluminium venetians or PVC verticals that handle direct water contact.

Do you service combined laundry-bathrooms?

Yes — many Melbourne homes have combined laundry-bathroom spaces. The same moisture-resistant products work perfectly. We'll assess the layout and recommend a consistent look across both functions of the room.
