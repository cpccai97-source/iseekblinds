Expert Advice

Allergy-Reducing Cleaning Tips

1

Weekly Wipe-Down

Use a damp microfibre cloth to wipe blinds weekly. Microfibre traps dust particles rather than spreading them into the air — a key difference from feather dusters.

2

Top-Down Approach

Always clean from top to bottom to prevent dust from settling on already-cleaned surfaces. This simple habit makes a noticeable difference for sensitive noses.

3

Mild Detergent Only

Harsh chemicals can leave residues that trigger sensitivity. Use a gentle, fragrance-free detergent diluted in warm water for the best results.

4

Ventilate During Cleaning

Open windows while cleaning to flush airborne particles outside. Even 10 minutes of fresh air circulation significantly reduces indoor allergen concentration.
