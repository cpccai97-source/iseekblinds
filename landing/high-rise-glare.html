The Problem

Life Above the 10th Floor 🌇

High-rise living comes with unique window challenges that ground-level homes never face.

☀️

Intense Direct Glare

No trees, no neighbouring buildings to filter light. Above floor 10, you're catching unobstructed sun from dawn to dusk — screens become unreadable, eyes strain constantly.

🌡️

Solar Heat Gain

Floor-to-ceiling glass acts like a greenhouse. North and west-facing apartments can spike 8–12°C above comfortable temperatures, pushing aircon bills through the roof.

🪟

Oversized Windows

Standard blinds don't fit 2.7m+ floor-to-ceiling panels. You need custom solutions designed for the scale and weight demands of high-rise glazing.

🔆

UV Damage

At elevation, UV intensity increases. Furniture, artwork, and flooring fade 40% faster without proper UV-blocking window treatments.

🏗️

Body Corp Restrictions

Many buildings mandate uniform external appearance. Your blinds must comply with body corporate colour and style requirements.

🔊

Wind & Noise

Higher floors mean more wind noise. The right blinds add an insulation layer that dampens external noise by up to 30%.
