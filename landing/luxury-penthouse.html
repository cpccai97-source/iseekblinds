Premium Services for Premium Spaces

Bespoke Design Consultation

Our senior designers visit your home with a curated collection of premium samples. We assess light patterns, architectural features, and your personal aesthetic to craft a tailored window furnishing scheme that elevates every room.

Motorised & Smart Home Integration

Seamless integration with Lutron, Somfy, and Apple HomeKit. Schedule your blinds to rise with the sunrise, close at dusk, or respond to voice commands. Silent motors with concealed hardware for a flawless finish.

Floor-to-Ceiling Solutions

Specialising in oversized and panoramic windows up to 6 metres. Our engineering team ensures smooth operation and perfect alignment for the most challenging installations in high-rise penthouses and double-height spaces.

Premium European Fabrics

Sourced from the finest mills in Italy, Belgium, and France. Silks, linens, wool blends, and performance textiles that drape beautifully and stand the test of time. Exclusive patterns not available through standard suppliers.

Architectural Shutters

Hand-crafted plantation shutters in premium hardwoods and engineered timber. Custom colour matching, split-tilt systems, and special shapes including arched, circular, and angled configurations for heritage and contemporary homes.

White-Glove Installation

Our installation team works to your schedule, with dust sheets, protective coverings, and meticulous cleanup as standard. Post-install walkthrough, programming assistance, and a 10-year warranty on all premium installations.
