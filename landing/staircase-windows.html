Why High Windows Are

The Solution

Smart Motorised
Window Systems

Our engineered motorised solutions eliminate the need to ever physically access your high windows. Control every blind from your phone, voice assistant, or automated schedule.

  • Somfy & Automate Motors — Premium whisper-quiet motors rated for 10+ years of daily operation. Battery or hardwired options.
  • Smart Home Integration — Works with Google Home, Alexa, Apple HomeKit, and all major smart home ecosystems.
  • Sun & Temperature Sensors — Automatic adjustment based on real-time light and heat. Your blinds respond before you even notice the glare.
  • Custom Engineering — Angled rails, curved tracks, and bespoke brackets for the most unusual window shapes in stairwells and voids.
  • Solar-Powered Option — No wiring needed. Our solar cells charge the motor battery — ideal for remote skylights where power isn't available.
Why Go Motorised

Safety Meets Convenience

01

Zero Ladder Risk

Eliminate the most dangerous task in home maintenance. Our motorised blinds are operated entirely from ground level — by remote, app, or voice command.

02

Child & Pet Safe

No dangling cords or chains. Motorised blinds are the safest option for families — fully compliant with Australian child safety standards AS/NZS 13120.

03

Set & Forget

Programme daily schedules: open at sunrise, close at sunset. Sun sensors react automatically. Your hard-to-reach windows manage themselves year-round.
