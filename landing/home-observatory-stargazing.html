Process

From Light Leak
to Dark Sky

Our observatory-specific installation process, refined over hundreds of astronomy spaces across Melbourne.

1

Dark Sky Assessment

We visit at night with a calibrated lux meter. We map every light source — windows, gaps, reflections, indicators — and measure your current darkness level.

💡 Pro tip: Note which direction your main observation window faces. We'll factor in seasonal moon paths and neighbouring light angles.
2

Custom Solution Design

Based on your assessment, we design a layered system: primary blackout, edge sealing, door airlock, and optional motorisation. Every millimetre is accounted for.

3

Precision Manufacture

Your blinds are fabricated to exact measurements with astronomy-grade blackout fabric, custom side channels, and your choice of operation method.

4

Install & Calibrate

Professional installation followed by a darkness calibration test. We won't leave until the lux meter reads ≤0.01 at every window. That's our guarantee.

🔭 We schedule final checks after sunset so you can test with your equipment before we leave.
