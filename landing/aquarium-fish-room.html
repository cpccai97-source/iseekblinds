Why Window Blinds Matter

Uncontrolled window light adds 6-8 hours of unpredictable spectrum to your tank every day. Even the best tank lighting becomes useless when ambient light overrides your photoperiod. Our blinds let you eliminate window interference while still enjoying natural light when you want it.
