Four Levels

Choose Your
Privacy Level

Every window has different needs. We offer four distinct privacy tiers, each engineered for specific light and visibility requirements.

◯

Sheer

15–25% Privacy

Softly diffused natural light flows through while gently obscuring interior views during the day. Ideal for living areas where connection to the outdoors matters.

◔

Light Filtering

50–70% Privacy

Blocks direct sightlines while preserving ambient glow. Perfect for kitchens, studies, and open-plan areas that need daytime privacy without darkness.

◑

Dimout

85–95% Privacy

Near-complete privacy with a soft ambient halo. Reduces glare and UV while maintaining a gentle sense of time — ideal for bedrooms and nurseries.

●

Blockout

100% Privacy

Total visual and light blockage. Zero light leakage when properly installed with side channels. Essential for shift workers, media rooms, and street-facing bedrooms.

Room by Room

Tailored
Recommendations

Each space in your home has unique privacy needs. Here's our expert guidance for every room.

🛏

Master Bedroom

Recommended: Blockout or Dimout. Pair blockout rollers with sheer curtains for a dual-layer system — total darkness for sleep, soft light when you wake. Consider motorised operation for effortless morning transitions.

🛋

Living Room

Recommended: Sheer or Light Filtering. Maintain visual connection with your garden or streetscape while softening incoming glare. Sunscreen fabrics with 1–5% openness factor provide privacy without enclosure.

🍳

Kitchen & Dining

Recommended: Light Filtering. Moisture-resistant fabrics that diffuse light evenly across bench surfaces. Venetian blinds offer adjustable slat angles for precise privacy control while cooking.

🚿

Bathroom

Recommended: Dimout or Blockout. Aluminium venetians or PVC shutters provide maximum privacy with full moisture resistance. Top-down/bottom-up blinds let in sky-light while blocking sightlines at body height.

💻

Home Office

Recommended: Light Filtering or Dimout. Reduce screen glare without creating a cave. Dual roller systems let you switch between focused work mode and bright break mode throughout the day.

👶

Children's Room

Recommended: Blockout with child-safe operation. Cordless or motorised blockout blinds ensure daytime nap darkness and nighttime security. All our children's room solutions meet Australian child safety standards.

Dual Function

Day & Night
Privacy

Your privacy needs change with the sun. Our dual-function systems adapt seamlessly from dawn to dusk.

Daytime Privacy

During daylight hours, interior light levels are lower than outside — making it naturally harder for passersby to see in. Our sheer and light-filtering options enhance this effect.

  • → Sunscreen rollers (1–5% openness)
  • → Sheer curtains with privacy weave
  • → Day/night zebra blinds
  • → Venetians angled for view control
  • → Top-down/bottom-up cellular

Nighttime Privacy

When interior lights are on and it's dark outside, roles reverse — inside becomes clearly visible. You need denser fabrics or layered treatments for evening privacy.

  • → Blockout roller behind sheer
  • → Lined curtains with thermal backing
  • → Honeycomb blinds (dual cell)
  • → Plantation shutters (closed position)
  • → Motorised scheduling at sunset

Benefits

Beyond Just
Privacy

The right window treatments deliver more than seclusion — they transform how your home feels.

01

UV Protection

Block up to 99% of harmful UV rays. Protect your furniture, artwork, and flooring from sun damage while maintaining the view you love.

02

Energy Savings

Properly fitted privacy blinds create an insulating air pocket against your glass, reducing heat transfer by up to 60% in summer and winter.

03

Noise Reduction

Honeycomb and heavy fabric options absorb sound from busy streets, neighbours, and traffic. Particularly effective with double-glazed windows.

04

Security

Prevent casual observation of your home's contents. Blockout blinds deter opportunistic theft by hiding valuables from street view entirely.

05

Sleep Quality

True blockout blinds with side channels eliminate all light ingress. Critical for shift workers, light-sensitive sleepers, and young children.

06

Property Value

Quality window furnishings are one of the top features buyers and renters look for. Our solutions add tangible value to your property.
