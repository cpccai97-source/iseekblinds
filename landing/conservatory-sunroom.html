Design

Style Your Sunroom

Function is essential — but your conservatory should also look stunning. Here's how to balance performance with aesthetics.

Colour Coordination

Choose blinds that complement your conservatory's architectural style. White and cream tones keep spaces feeling open and airy, while earth tones add warmth to modern glass structures.

  • → Victorian conservatory: timber venetians or Roman blinds
  • → Modern lean-to: minimal roller in neutral tones
  • → Orangery style: plantation shutters for elegance
  • → Contemporary glass box: motorised sheers, no visible hardware

Material Selection

Natural materials like bamboo and timber add organic warmth to glass-heavy spaces. Technical fabrics offer superior performance with minimal visual weight. Mix materials for depth.

Layered Approach

Combine roof blinds with side wall treatments for complete control. Use sheer layers for daytime beauty and add blockout for summer heat or privacy when entertaining in the evening.
