Engineered for Creativity

Every creative space has unique lighting demands. We solve them all.

🎨

UV Protection

Block 99% of harmful UV rays to prevent fading, discolouration and degradation of paintings, photographs, sculptures and mixed-media works.

💡

Light Diffusion

Transform harsh sunlight into soft, even illumination. Sheer and translucent fabrics create gallery-quality ambient light without hotspots.

🌑

Total Blackout

Complete darkness on demand for photography studios, projection rooms and light-sensitive installations. Zero light leakage guaranteed.

🎛️

Motorised Control

Programmable scenes via app, voice or wall switch. Set different lighting presets for exhibitions, open days and private viewings.

🔇

Acoustic Dampening

Heavy-weave fabrics reduce echo and reverb in open gallery spaces, improving sound quality for events, talks and performances.

🧊

Climate Stability

Honeycomb blinds create an insulating air pocket to stabilise temperature — critical for preserving temperature-sensitive artworks.
