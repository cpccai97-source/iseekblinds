Colour Sets the Mood.
We Help You Get It Right.

Whether you're starting fresh or refreshing a single room, the right window colour transforms a space. We've curated five palettes that are defining Australian interiors in 2026 — and every one is available across our full blinds, curtains, and shutters range.

01 Trending

Sunbaked Terracotta

Warm, earthy, and inviting. Terracotta tones bring an organic richness to living rooms and bedrooms. Pair with cream sheers or natural timber venetians for a layered, sophisticated look.

02 Most Popular

Serene Sage

The undeniable colour of 2026. Sage green delivers calm and balance — perfect for bedrooms, studies, and nurseries. Our roller blinds and honeycomb range offer multiple sage tones from soft mint to deep eucalyptus.

03 Timeless

Deep Coastal Blue

Moving beyond beachy pastels — 2026's blues are deeper and more dramatic. Think navy shutters, ocean-toned S-fold curtains, and midnight roller blinds. Stunning in bathrooms and open-plan living.

04 Warm & Versatile

Golden Sandstone

Neutral but never boring. Golden sandstone tones add warmth without overwhelming a room. The go-to for open-plan homes where multiple windows need cohesion. Available in our full venetian and curtain ranges.

05 Bold Choice

Dusty Plum

For those who want something unexpected. Muted plum and mauve tones add a touch of luxury without going full drama. Stunning in bedrooms with blockout roller blinds or velvet pinch-pleat curtains.

How to Choose Your Perfect Colour

🎨

Start With Your Walls

Your wall colour is your anchor. Choose blinds that complement — not match — for depth and visual interest.

☀️

Consider the Light

North-facing rooms can handle deeper tones. South-facing rooms benefit from warmer, lighter shades to prevent feeling cold.

📐

Think in Layers

Combine a sheer curtain with a blockout roller for both colour play and function. Two tones beat one.

🏠

Match Your Style

Modern minimalist? Stick to one palette. Eclectic? Mix two trending palettes for a bold, curated look.

🧪

Test Before You Commit

Order free fabric samples — colours look different under your home's lighting vs. a screen.

💬

Get Expert Advice

Our in-home consultants bring fabric swatches and help you find the perfect shade. It's free, no pressure.
