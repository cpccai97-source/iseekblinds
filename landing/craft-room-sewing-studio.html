Every Maker Knows This Pain ✂️

🎨

Colour Shifts

You match fabric colours under craft room light, then they look completely different in the finished piece. Harsh direct sun washes everything out; too dim and you can't distinguish navy from black.

😵‍💫

Eye Strain

Hours of close-up detail work — embroidery, beading, small seams — with glare on your fabric or screen. By afternoon your eyes are done, but your project isn't.

🌡️

Heat Build-Up

North-facing rooms bake in summer. Your iron is already adding heat. Fabric warps, glue dries too fast, and you're sweating onto your work surface.

🧵

Fading Materials

UV ruins stored fabric, thread colours, and finished pieces on display. That quilt you spent 200 hours on shouldn't lose its vibrancy in a sunlit room.
