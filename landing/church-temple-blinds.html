Cathedral windows reaching 8+ metres. Standard solutions don't exist. We engineer motorised systems built to the exact dimensions of your arched, pointed, or rose windows.

Heritage overlays, council restrictions, conservation architects. We navigate the approvals process and use reversible, non-invasive installation methods that protect original fabric.

Motors that whisper, not whir. Fabric that absorbs rather than reflects. Every element is chosen so your blinds enhance the acoustic character of the space, never disrupt it.

Heritage Preservation
"The light through those windows has told our story for a hundred years. We needed someone who understood that."

Melbourne's sacred buildings span bluestone Gothic Revival churches from the 1860s to modernist temples from the 1970s. Each has unique heritage requirements.

Our approach is always conservation-first: reversible fixings, no drilling into heritage stonework, colour-matched components that disappear into the architecture. We work alongside your conservation architect — not against them.

Every installation is documented with before/after photography and a detailed methods statement, so your heritage file stays complete.
