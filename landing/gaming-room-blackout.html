Why blackout matters

Every Photon Counts

🎯

Screen Glare

Even minor reflections reduce contrast ratios by up to 40%. In competitive gaming, that's the difference between spotting an enemy or missing them entirely. True blackout eliminates every unwanted light source.

😴

Eye Fatigue

Mismatched ambient light forces your pupils to constantly adjust, causing strain during long sessions. A properly dark room lets your eyes relax and focus on what matters — your screen.

🎙️

Stream Quality

Inconsistent lighting ruins webcam footage and creates harsh shadows. Controlled darkness means your ring light or key light does all the work — clean, professional streaming every time.

🌡️

Heat Control

Gaming rigs generate serious heat. Direct sunlight through windows can push room temps up 5-8°C. Blackout blinds reflect solar heat, keeping your setup and your GPU cooler.

🔊

Noise Reduction

Heavy blackout fabrics absorb sound, reducing outside noise by up to 7dB. Fewer distractions during clutch moments. Your callouts stay clear, ambient noise stays out.

🏠

Dual Purpose

When you're not gaming, motorised blinds open fully with one tap. Your room transforms back to a bright, liveable space in seconds. No permanent cave vibes required.
