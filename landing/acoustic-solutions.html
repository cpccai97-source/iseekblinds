How Acoustic Blinds Work

Multi-layer technology that absorbs, blocks, and dampens sound waves

🔊

Sound Absorption

Dense, multi-layered fabrics absorb sound energy as it passes through, converting it to negligible heat rather than letting it bounce into your room.

🛡️

Mass Barrier

Heavier fabric weights create a mass-loaded barrier at the window, blocking airborne noise from passing through — similar to how a solid wall works.

🫧

Air Gap Dampening

The air gap between the blind and glass acts as an additional dampening layer, further reducing sound transmission with proper seal installation.
