Expert Advice

Nursery Window Tips from Our Specialists

01

Layer for Flexibility

Combine a sheer for daytime soft light with a blackout blind behind it. This gives you full control over nursery lighting at every hour.

02

Consider Orientation

East-facing rooms need strong blackout for morning naps. West-facing rooms benefit from UV-blocking fabric to keep the afternoon heat out.

03

Future-Proof the Design

Choose neutral colours and timeless styles. A nursery becomes a toddler room fast — pick coverings that grow with your child.
