Frequently Asked

Common questions from landlords and tenants

Can tenants install blinds without landlord permission?+
We offer non-invasive mounting options (tension fit, adhesive brackets) that don't require drilling. For standard installations, we recommend getting written approval from your landlord or property manager. We can provide documentation explaining the installation process to help with approval.
Do you offer bulk discounts for multiple properties?+
Yes! We offer tiered bulk pricing: 10% off for 3-4 properties, 15% off for 5-9 properties, and 20% off for 10+ properties. Property managers with ongoing needs can negotiate custom rates with a dedicated account manager.
How quickly can you fit out a property between tenants?+
We understand vacancy costs money. Our average turnaround from measure to install is 5 business days. For urgent jobs, we offer an express service (2-3 days) for a small surcharge. We can coordinate with property managers and real estate agents directly.
What if a tenant damages the blinds?+
Our rental-grade blinds use reinforced components designed to handle higher wear. Our warranty covers manufacturing defects. For accidental damage, we offer replacement components at cost price for our landlord clients. We also provide condition reports for bond purposes.
Are your blinds compliant with rental standards?+
All our blinds meet Australian safety standards. Child-safe options (cordless, wand-operated) are available and recommended for family rentals. We ensure all installations comply with Victorian rental property standards and can provide compliance certificates.
