Expert Tips for Granny Flat Windows

1

Go Ceiling-Mounted

Ceiling-recessed tracks make rooms feel taller and don't interfere with furniture placement below windows.

2

Light Colours Expand

White and neutral tones on blinds reflect light deeper into rooms, making them feel larger and airier.

3

Double Up Strategically

Pair a sheer with a blockout roller for bedrooms — daytime privacy plus nighttime darkness without bulk.

4

Insulate the Weakest Link

Windows lose 40% of heating/cooling. Honeycomb blinds act as a thermal blanket — pay for themselves in energy savings.
