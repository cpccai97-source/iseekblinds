🏠 Curated Collection

Products for the Nordic Home

Curtains

Linen Sheer Curtains

Soft, flowing linen in white, oat or fog grey. The quintessential Scandinavian window treatment — effortless, breathable and full of texture.

  • 100% European linen
  • S-fold or pinch pleat heading
  • Light filtering with UV protection
  • Machine washable
Venetian Blinds

Natural Timber Venetians

Basswood or paulownia timber in white wash, birch or oak tones. Precise light control with a warm, tactile presence.

  • 50mm or 63mm slat widths
  • Sustainably sourced timber
  • Cord-free options available
  • Custom stain matching
Honeycomb

Cellular Honeycomb Blinds

The Scandinavian obsession with warmth meets window coverings. Trapped air cells provide insulation while maintaining a clean, minimal profile.

  • Superior thermal insulation
  • Top-down/bottom-up operation
  • Neutral matte colour range
  • Motorised or cordless
Shutters

White Plantation Shutters

The ultimate Scandi window treatment. Crisp white shutters frame your windows like architecture — permanent, sculptural and timeless.

  • PVC or timber construction
  • 89mm or 114mm blades
  • Hidden tilt rod option
  • 25-year warranty

🏡 Room by Room

Scandinavian Styling Ideas

🛋️

Living Room

Floor-to-ceiling sheer curtains in white linen. Let the space breathe. Add a single roller blind behind for evening privacy.

↳ Recommended: Linen Sheers + Blockout Roller
🛏️

Bedroom

Layer a sheer curtain over honeycomb blinds for the ultimate Nordic bedroom. Soft light by day, complete blackout by night.

↳ Recommended: Honeycomb + Sheer Overlay
🍳

Kitchen

Timber venetians in white wash or birch. Easy to clean, great light control while cooking, and they add warmth above the sink.

↳ Recommended: 50mm Timber Venetians
🛁

Bathroom

PVC shutters in arctic white. Waterproof, mould-resistant and architecturally stunning. The closest thing to a Scandinavian spa at home.

↳ Recommended: PVC Plantation Shutters
📚

Study / Home Office

Sunscreen roller in grey or white. Reduces glare on screens while keeping the view. Minimal, functional, distraction-free.

↳ Recommended: 3% Sunscreen Roller
🚪

Hallway & Entry

Keep it clean. A single Roman blind or top-down honeycomb lets light in from above while shielding the front door from street view.

↳ Recommended: Top-Down Honeycomb
