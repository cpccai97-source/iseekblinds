The Challenge

Water Living Demands
Extraordinary Blinds

🧂

Salt Corrosion

Standard aluminium and steel components rust within months. Our marine-grade fittings use 316 stainless steel and anodised aluminium — the same spec used on ocean vessels.

💧

Constant Humidity

Condensation warps timber slats and breeds mould in fabric. We use moisture-wicking synthetics and PVC composites that laugh at 95% humidity days.

☀️

Relentless UV

Water reflection doubles UV intensity. Our fabrics block up to 99% of UV rays while preserving your harbour panorama — because nobody moves waterside to stare at walls.

📐

Non-Standard Windows

Porthole shapes, curved glass, sliding hatches — boats and waterfront builds rarely have standard openings. Every one of our installs is custom-measured and fabricated.
