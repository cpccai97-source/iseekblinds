Common Questions

Can arched windows have operable blinds?

Yes — plantation shutters with hinged panels and honeycomb blinds with fan-top options allow you to open and close the covering on arched windows.

How accurate does the measurement need to be?

Extremely. That's why we create physical templates for every specialty window. We never rely on standard measurements for unusual shapes.

Are specialty shapes more expensive?

Custom shapes do cost more than standard rectangles due to the templating and fabrication involved, but we keep pricing competitive. We'll quote upfront with no surprises.

How long does it take?

Typically 4-6 weeks from template to installation, depending on the complexity and product chosen. We'll give you exact timelines at consultation.
