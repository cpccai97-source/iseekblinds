The Challenges

Problems We Solve On The Road

01 🌡️

Heat Buildup

Metal bodies turn into ovens. Our thermal-backed fabrics reflect up to 85% of solar heat, keeping interior temps comfortable without running the AC flat out.

02 🔩

Odd Window Shapes

Curved corners, angled skylights, pop-top hatches — standard blinds won't cut it. We template every window and fabricate to the exact profile.

03 🛣️

Road Vibration

Rattling and bouncing destroy flimsy fittings. Our mounting systems use vibration-dampening brackets engineered for corrugated roads and highway travel.

04 🌙

Total Blackout

Park under a streetlight? No problem. Channel-guided blackout blinds seal every edge for complete darkness — essential for quality sleep away from home.

05 💨

Weight Matters

Every gram counts for towing. Our aluminium-tube rollers and lightweight honeycomb cells add minimal payload while maxing out insulation performance.

06 🏕️

Privacy at Camp

Close quarters at caravan parks mean neighbours are a metre away. One-way vision fabrics maintain views out while blocking sight in — day and night.
