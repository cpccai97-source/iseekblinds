The Big Window Challenge

Standard off-the-shelf blinds won't cut it. Large openings present unique problems that need custom solutions.

☀️

Heat & Glare

Large glass areas intensify sun exposure, driving up cooling costs and creating harsh glare that makes rooms unusable during peak hours.

📐

Non-Standard Sizes

Sliding doors, bi-folds and floor-to-ceiling windows rarely come in standard dimensions. Every millimetre matters for a clean, professional fit.

🏋️

Weight & Operation

Wide-span blinds and curtains are heavy. Manual operation becomes impractical — and mechanisms must handle the load reliably for years.

👁️

Privacy Without Compromise

You want the view and the light, but not the neighbours watching. Balancing openness with privacy is the eternal challenge.

🌡️

Insulation

Glass is a poor insulator. Large windows mean significant energy loss in winter and heat gain in summer without proper window treatments.

🎨

Design Continuity

Oversized windows are a design feature. Your blinds need to enhance the architecture, not fight it. Scale and proportion are critical.
