WHY IT MATTERS

6 Reasons Your Gym Needs Proper Blinds

A home gym is an investment. The right window coverings make the difference between a good workout space and a great one.

01

Glare on Screens

Following a Peloton class or YouTube workout? Screen glare kills your focus. Sunscreen blinds eliminate reflection while keeping natural light.

02

Temperature Control

Direct sun turns your gym into a sauna. Our solar reflective blinds can reduce room temperature by up to 8°C on hot days.

03

Mirror Reflection

Wall mirrors + uncontrolled sunlight = blinding reflections. Position-controlled blinds let you manage light angles precisely.

04

Moisture & Sweat

Intense workouts create humidity. Standard fabric blinds absorb moisture and develop mould. Our gym-rated fabrics resist it all.

05

Privacy

Nobody wants an audience during their deadlift PR attempt. Switchable privacy — open for light, closed for focus.

06

Ambience Control

HIIT needs bright energy. Yoga needs calm darkness. Motorised blinds with presets let you set the mood in seconds.
