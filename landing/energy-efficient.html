How Window Furnishings Save Energy

Your windows are responsible for up to 40% of your home's energy loss. Here's how the right blinds fight back.

❄️

Winter Insulation

Honeycomb blinds trap air in their cells, creating an insulating barrier that keeps warm air inside and cold air out.

☀️

Summer Heat Rejection

Reflective-backed roller blinds and external awnings block solar heat before it enters your room, reducing AC costs.

🔇

Draught Sealing

Fitted shutters and side-channel roller blinds seal the window frame, stopping cold draughts from creeping in.

🌙

Light Control

Blockout blinds prevent heat loss through glass at night while keeping bedrooms dark for better sleep.

🤖

Smart Automation

Motorised blinds with sun sensors adjust automatically throughout the day — maximum efficiency with zero effort.

🏡

Layered Systems

Combine sheers + blockout for all-day efficiency: filter light during the day, insulate at night.
