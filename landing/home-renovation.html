The Renovation Timeline

When to plan, measure, and install window furnishings during your renovation — so everything lands on time.

1
Week 1–2 • Planning Phase

Design Consultation

Book a free consultation as soon as your renovation plans are finalised. We'll review architectural drawings, suggest window treatment options, and coordinate with your builder on power points for motorised blinds or curtain tracks recessed into bulkheads.

2
Week 3–4 • Pre-Construction

Electrical & Structural Prep

If you're going motorised, now is when your electrician runs power to window locations. For plantation shutters, we confirm frame depths. For ceiling-mounted curtain tracks, your plasterer needs to know placement before the ceiling goes up.

3
Week 6–8 • During Build

Fabric & Material Selection

Once your paint colours, flooring, and cabinetry are locked in, we bring samples to your site for colour matching under your actual lighting. This ensures perfect coordination — not guesswork from a showroom visit.

4
Week 10–12 • Post-Plaster

Final Measure

Once plastering and painting are complete, we do the final measure. Windows must be in their finished state — no renders, architraves, or trim still to come. This is the "measure once, fit perfectly" stage.

5
Week 14–16 • Completion

Professional Installation

Your blinds, curtains, or shutters arrive and are installed in a single day. We coordinate with your builder to ensure we're the last trade in — after cleaning, after floors, after everything. Move-in ready.

Builder Install vs Retrofit

Two paths to beautiful windows. Here's how to choose the right one for your project.

New Build / Major Reno

Builder Coordination

We work directly with your builder from day one. Window furnishings are designed into the build — not bolted on after.

  • ✦ Recessed curtain tracks in bulkheads
  • ✦ Pre-wired motorisation at every window
  • ✦ Shutter frames built into window reveals
  • ✦ Coordinated with plasterers & electricians
  • ✦ Single defects period covers everything
  • ✦ Builder-direct invoicing available
Existing Home

Retrofit Installation

Already renovated or refreshing specific rooms? We measure around your existing finishes and install with zero disruption.

  • ✦ No structural changes required
  • ✦ Surface-mounted or face-fixed options
  • ✦ Battery-powered motorisation available
  • ✦ Room-by-room or whole-home packages
  • ✦ Same-day install for most standard blinds
  • ✦ Flexible payment plans available

Renovation Checklist

Don't miss a step. Your complete window furnishing checklist for renovations.

  • ✓

    Include window treatments in your renovation budget

    Allocate 3–5% of total renovation cost. Better to plan upfront than scramble at the end.

  • ✓

    Book consultation before construction begins

    This lets us coordinate with your builder on wiring, frames, and track placement.

  • ✓

    Confirm power points for motorised options

    Your electrician needs window locations marked. We provide a spec sheet for your sparky.

  • ✓

    Select fabrics after paint colours are finalised

    Matching under artificial showroom light ≠ matching under your actual room light. We bring samples to site.

  • ✓

    Schedule final measure after plastering & painting

    Windows must be in their finished state. Even 5mm of render can affect shutter fit.

  • ✓

    Install window furnishings last

    After floors, after painting, after cleaning. This protects your investment from construction dust and damage.
