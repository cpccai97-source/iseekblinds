Common Questions

Everything you need to know about bi-fold door blinds

Can I still open my bi-fold doors with the blinds installed?
Absolutely. Roller blinds mount above the doors and roll up completely clear. Panel glides stack to one side. Both options allow full, unobstructed door operation.
What's the maximum width you can cover?
Single motorised rollers cover up to 4.5m. For wider openings, we use linked systems — two or more blinds sharing a single control — covering openings up to 8m+ seamlessly.
Do I need an electrician for motorised blinds?
Not necessarily. Our rechargeable battery motors (charge lasts 6–12 months) require zero wiring. Hard-wired options are available if you prefer permanent power — we coordinate with your electrician.
What about insulation for large glass areas?
Honeycomb blinds are our top recommendation for thermal performance. The cellular construction creates air pockets that insulate against both heat and cold — crucial for Melbourne's variable climate.
How long does installation take?
Most bi-fold door installations are completed in 1–2 hours per opening. Hardwired motorised systems may take slightly longer for cable routing and setup.
