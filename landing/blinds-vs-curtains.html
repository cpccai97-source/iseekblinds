The Quick Overview

Both blinds and curtains have their strengths. Here's how they stack up at a glance.

Blinds

Precise & Modern

Blinds offer clean lines, precise light control, and a contemporary aesthetic. They're ideal for kitchens, bathrooms and offices where moisture resistance and easy cleaning matter.

  • ✓ Precise light control with adjustable slats
  • ✓ Easy to clean — wipe or dust
  • ✓ Space-saving, sits flush to the window
  • ✓ Moisture-resistant options available
  • ✓ Modern, minimal aesthetic
  • ✓ Wide range: roller, venetian, honeycomb
Curtains

Elegant & Warm

Curtains bring softness, warmth and a sense of luxury to any room. They're unmatched for insulation, acoustic dampening and creating a cosy atmosphere in living and bedrooms.

  • ✓ Superior thermal & acoustic insulation
  • ✓ Adds texture, colour and visual warmth
  • ✓ Makes rooms feel grander
  • ✓ Wide fabric and pattern choices
  • ✓ Great for large & odd-shaped windows
  • ✓ Can be layered with sheers
The best window treatment isn't about trends — it's about how you live in the space.

Room-by-Room Recommendations

Every room has different needs. Here's our expert pick for each space.

🛋️
Our Pick: Curtains + Sheers

Living Room

Layer sheer curtains with blockout curtains for maximum versatility — warmth, style and light control in one.

🛏️
Our Pick: Blockout Roller Blinds

Bedroom

Roller blinds with blockout fabric ensure darkness for better sleep. Add curtains for a luxe layered look.

🍳
Our Pick: Venetian Blinds

Kitchen

Aluminium or faux-wood venetians handle steam and splashes easily, with great light control while cooking.

🚿
Our Pick: PVC Blinds

Bathroom

Moisture-resistant PVC or aluminium blinds. Curtains and fabric options will deteriorate in humid conditions.

💼
Our Pick: Sunscreen Rollers

Home Office

Reduce glare on screens while maintaining the view. Sunscreen roller blinds are the clear winner here.

🧸
Our Pick: Cordless Honeycomb

Children's Room

Child-safe cordless honeycomb blinds provide blockout, insulation and safety — all in one product.

Why Not Both?

The biggest trend in window furnishings right now? Layering. Combine roller blinds with sheer curtains, or pair honeycomb blinds with s-fold curtains for the ultimate in style, light control and insulation. At iSeekBlinds, we specialise in creating the perfect combination for your home.
