Blind Solutions for Your Doors

Every door type demands a different approach. Here are our most popular solutions, each tailored for smooth operation and a flawless look.

01

Roller Blinds

Slim-profile rollers mount neatly above your French doors or within the reveal of sliding doors. Choose blockout for bedrooms, sunscreen for living areas, or dual rollers for ultimate versatility. Cordless spring or motorised operation keeps the look clean and child-safe.

02

Sheer Curtains & Drapes

Full-length sheers frame your doors elegantly while filtering harsh sunlight. Pair with blockout curtains for a layered look. S-fold or pinch-pleat heading on a ceiling-mounted track keeps the profile sleek and doesn't interfere with door handles.

03

Plantation Shutters

Hinged or bi-fold shutters mounted directly to the door frame — they swing with your French doors. Adjustable louvres let you control light and airflow precisely. Our basswood and PVC options suit every budget and style.

04

Panel Glide Systems

Wide fabric panels that slide along a top track — perfect for covering large patio or sliding doors. Panels stack neatly behind each other, giving you full access. Available in blockout, light-filtering, and sunscreen fabrics.

05

Vertical Blinds

Classic vertical louvres draw to one side for easy passage through sliding doors. Modern fabric options look far more refined than old-style PVC. Chainless bottom weights and wand control keep them child-safe and tangle-free.

06

Motorised Solutions

One-touch or voice-activated blinds for your doors. Integrate with smart home systems (Google, Alexa, Apple HomeKit) to control light and privacy without lifting a finger. No cords, no chains — just effortless operation.

Child-Safe & Cordless Door Blinds

French doors and patio doors are high-traffic zones — especially for little ones. That's why every solution we offer prioritises child safety without compromising on style or function.

Australian safety standards require all new blind installations to comply with cord-safety regulations. We go further:

  • Spring-assisted cordless roller blinds — lift and lower with a gentle push
  • Motorised operation via remote, wall switch, or smartphone app
  • Wand-operated verticals — no chains or cords at child height
  • Breakaway chain connectors on all remaining corded products
  • Integrated cord guides that pin cords flush to the wall
  • Plantation shutters with no cords at all — just tilt the louvres by hand

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about blinds for French doors, patio doors, and sliding glass doors.

Can I put blinds on French doors that open inward?

Yes! We use slim-profile roller blinds or plantation shutters that mount within the door frame. They sit flush against the glass so the door swings freely without catching. Magnetic hold-down brackets keep the blind in place when the door is tilted.

What's the best blind for large sliding patio doors?

Panel glide systems and motorised roller blinds are our top recommendations. Panel glides cover spans up to 6 metres and stack neatly to one side. Motorised rollers give you one-touch operation — ideal for wide, heavy-coverage situations.

Are your door blinds child-safe?

Absolutely. All our door blind products comply with Australian child safety standards. We offer cordless spring operation, motorised systems, and wand-controlled verticals. No dangling cords or chains near high-traffic doorways.

Can I motorise my French door blinds?

Yes. We install rechargeable battery-powered motors (no wiring needed) or hardwired motors for new builds. They work with Google Home, Alexa, Apple HomeKit, and most home automation systems. Set schedules or control via your phone.

How much do French door blinds cost?

Pricing depends on the product type, fabric choice, and door size. Roller blinds start from around $180 per door panel, panel glides from $350 per set, and plantation shutters from $450 per door. We provide free, no-obligation quotes with exact pricing.

Do you offer blinds for bi-fold doors?

Yes — roller blinds and curtains work brilliantly with bi-fold doors. We mount above the opening so the blinds sit independently of the door panels. When the bi-folds are stacked open, the blinds retract out of sight. See our bi-fold door blinds page for details.

How long does installation take?

Typically 2–4 weeks from measure to installation. The measure and quote appointment takes about 30 minutes, and installation day is usually 1–3 hours depending on the number of doors. We work around your schedule.

Can you match blinds to my existing window treatments?

Absolutely. We carry hundreds of fabrics and can match or complement your existing roller blinds, curtains, or shutters. Bring a photo or swatch to your consultation and we'll find the perfect match.
