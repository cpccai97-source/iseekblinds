The Problem

Melbourne's UV Is No Joke

Australia has some of the highest UV levels in the world. Without proper window protection, your home suffers in ways you might not see — until it's too late.

🪑

Furniture Fading

UV rays cause sofas, carpets, timber floors and artwork to fade and deteriorate within months of unprotected sun exposure.

🌡️

Extreme Heat Gain

North and west-facing windows can push room temperatures 8-12°C higher than ambient, overworking your air conditioning.

👁️

Glare & Discomfort

Harsh afternoon glare makes rooms unusable — screens unreadable, eyes strained, and spaces uncomfortable for hours.
