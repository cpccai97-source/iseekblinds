How It Works

From rooftop visit to shade — in 4 steps

Rooftop Assessment

We visit your rooftop, assess the structure, measure everything, check wind exposure, drainage and fixings. Takes about 30 minutes. Always free.

Our designer creates a 3D mockup showing exactly how your shade solution will look. You choose fabrics, colours and features.
