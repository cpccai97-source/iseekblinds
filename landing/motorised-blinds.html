Frequently Asked Questions

How much do motorised blinds cost?
Motorisation typically adds $150–$350 per blind depending on motor type and size. Battery motors are the most affordable retrofit option. We provide detailed quotes during our free consultation with no obligation.
Can I motorise my existing blinds?
In many cases, yes. Battery-powered retrofit motors can be fitted to existing roller blinds. However, for the best results and warranty coverage, we often recommend new motorised blinds with integrated motors.
Are motorised blinds noisy?
Modern motors are remarkably quiet—typically under 35dB, which is quieter than a whisper. Premium brands like Somfy and Acmeda have invested heavily in silent motor technology.
Do they work with Google Home / Alexa / HomeKit?
Yes. Most of our motorised solutions integrate with major smart home platforms. Wi-Fi-enabled motors connect directly. Bluetooth motors typically need a bridge/hub for voice assistant integration.
What happens during a power outage?
Battery and solar motors continue working normally. Hardwired motors can still be manually operated via the manual override mechanism. All our motorised products include manual backup.
Can I set schedules and timers?
Absolutely. Most motorised systems support scheduling via their app—wake up with blinds opening at sunrise, close for privacy at dusk. Sun sensors can also automate based on light levels.
