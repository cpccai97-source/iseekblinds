The Challenge

Why Standard Blinds Don't Work

Double glazed windows require specialist solutions. Here's what you need to know.

🔩

Drilling Risks

Drilling into double glazed frames can break the seal, void your warranty, and compromise thermal performance. Our solutions require zero drilling.

📏

Shallow Frame Depth

Many double glazed windows have shallow reveals that won't accommodate standard brackets. We use ultra-slim mounting systems designed for tight spaces.

🌡️

Heat Build-Up

Incorrect blind types can trap heat between the glass layers, potentially damaging seals. We select materials that work with your glazing, not against it.

🔒

Warranty Concerns

Most window manufacturers void warranties if frames are drilled. Our perfect fit and clip-in systems keep your warranty fully intact.

FAQ

Common Questions

Will blinds void my double glazing warranty?

Not with our perfect fit systems. They clip into the glazing bead without any screws or drilling, keeping your warranty fully intact. We provide documentation confirming our installation method is non-invasive.

Can I still open tilt-and-turn windows with blinds fitted?

Absolutely. Perfect fit blinds move with the window sash, so you can tilt and turn as normal. The blind stays in place regardless of window position.

Will blinds cause condensation or heat damage?

We carefully select materials and colours that won't cause excessive heat build-up between the glass panes. Our specialist advice ensures the right product for your specific glazing system.

What if my window frames are very shallow?

Our perfect fit systems are designed for frames as shallow as 15mm. We also offer face-fix adhesive options for ultra-narrow profiles, still without drilling.

Can I remove the blinds myself if needed?

Yes — perfect fit blinds unclip in seconds with no tools required. This is ideal for cleaning or if you're a renter who needs to remove them when moving out.
