CH 01 — The Problem

Every Studio Has
Window Problems

Windows are the weakest link in any recording chain. Here's what they're doing to your audio and video.

CH 01 🔊 Sound Leak

External noise bleeds into recordings — traffic, birds, neighbours. A single-pane window blocks only 20-25 dB.

CH 02 💡 Glare & Hotspots

Uncontrolled daylight creates blown-out backgrounds on camera and harsh reflections on screens and monitors.

CH 03 🔁 Reverb & Echo

Hard glass surfaces reflect mid-to-high frequencies, adding unwanted room reverb that muddles vocal clarity.

CH 04 Heat & HVAC Noise 🌡️

Sun-heated rooms force AC to work harder — and that hum shows up on sensitive condenser microphones.
