Office Setup Tips

Get the most from your window treatments with smart desk positioning.

01

Face Perpendicular to Windows

Position your desk so windows are to the side, not behind or in front. Reduces direct glare and creates even lighting for video calls.

02

Use Sunscreen, Not Blockout

Complete darkness causes eye strain under artificial light. Sunscreen fabric lets in diffused natural light — better for focus and mood.

03

Automate with Schedules

Motorised blinds can open at 8am and adjust for afternoon sun automatically. One less thing to think about during deep work.

04

Layer for Acoustics

Add heavy curtains over blinds for sound dampening. Honeycomb blinds also trap air and reduce noise. Essential for street-facing offices.
