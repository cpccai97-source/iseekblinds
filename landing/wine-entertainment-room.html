🍾 Wine Cellar Solutions

Protect Your Collection.
In Every Condition.

A great wine cellar is about far more than storage. UV radiation, temperature swings, and humidity fluctuations are the silent enemies of any fine collection. Our specialised wine cellar blinds create an optimal micro-environment — protecting both your investment and the integrity of every bottle.

  • ☀️

    UV-Blocking Fabrics

    Ultraviolet light degrades tannins, strips colour, and accelerates premature ageing in wine. Our UV-blocking fabrics filter up to 99% of harmful UV rays through glass doors and any windows adjacent to your cellar — preserving the chemical integrity of every vintage in your collection.

  • 🌡️

    Temperature Regulation

    Consistent temperature is critical for long-term storage. Our honeycomb and thermal-backed blinds add a significant insulation layer to glass surfaces, reducing temperature variation by up to 8°C compared to unshaded glass — helping your cellar maintain the ideal 12–14°C storage window without overworking your climate system.

  • 💧

    Humidity-Resistant Materials

    Wine cellars are deliberately kept humid to preserve cork integrity. Our moisture-resistant roller and screen blinds are manufactured from materials that resist mould, mildew, and warping in high-humidity environments — unlike standard blinds that can deteriorate within months in cellar conditions.

  • 🔇

    Vibration Dampening

    Vibration disturbs the sediment in aged wines and disrupts the slow chemical processes of maturation. Blinds and window furnishings at glass surfaces help dampen external vibration transmission — a consideration often overlooked but appreciated by serious collectors.

Set the Mood.
Every Time.

The perfect home bar is about atmosphere — and nothing destroys ambience faster than harsh afternoon glare or unwanted visibility from neighbours. Day/night blinds, layered sheers, and adjustable louvres let you dial in the exact light level — bright and welcoming for afternoon events, or dramatically dim for evening cocktail hours. Our light-filtering fabrics create a warm, diffused glow that flatters both guests and your décor.

🎬 Home Theatre Integration

Cinema-Grade Darkness.
At Home.

A premium home theatre demands total light control, acoustic enhancement, and seamless smart-home integration. Our home theatre blind solutions are specified to commercial cinema standards — engineered to eliminate every source of ambient light while enhancing the acoustic properties of your screening room.

⬛

Total Blackout System

Our blockout roller blinds achieve a true 100% blackout with cassette-and-channel side-channel systems that eliminate every sliver of light from the edges — the failure point of most standard blockout blinds. Zero light bleed means maximum contrast and a genuine cinematic experience even in south-facing rooms with afternoon sun.

🔊

Acoustic Enhancement

Hard surfaces reflect sound and create flutter echo — the enemy of dialogue clarity in a home cinema. Our heavy-weight curtains and acoustic-rated blinds add absorption at the most acoustically significant surface in the room: the glass. Combined with our curtain track systems, they can reduce reverberation time by up to 15% in a typical media room.

🏠

Smart Home Integration

Program your blinds into movie-watching scenes: one press dims the lights, closes the blinds, and lowers the projector screen simultaneously. Works seamlessly with Lutron, Control4, Crestron, Savant, Home Assistant, Apple HomeKit, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa. Wired and wireless options available for new-builds and retrofits.

🎞️

Projection-Friendly Fabrics

Dark, matte fabrics on side walls and across window bays eliminate reflective surfaces that can wash out projected images. Our dark-coloured velvet-finish and textured woven fabrics absorb stray light — improving perceived contrast and colour accuracy on both projector screens and large-format LED displays.

⚡

Motorised Precision

Manual blinds in a dedicated cinema room break the experience. Our motorised systems operate silently — DC motor-driven with decibel levels below ambient HVAC noise — so nothing disturbs the opening credits. Recessed cassettes and concealed wiring keep the aesthetic clean and professional.

🛋️

Multi-Zone Scene Control

Create distinct scenes for different content types: bright for sport, dim for drama, full blackout for film. Our multi-zone motorised systems allow individual control of each window or linked group control of all windows simultaneously — adjustable from your remote, phone, or integrated into your whole-home automation system.
