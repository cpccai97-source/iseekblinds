6 Smart Ways to Save on Blinds

Our budget experts share insider tips to maximise your dollar without sacrificing style.

1

Bundle More Windows

Our per-window price drops significantly when you order 8+ windows together. A whole-home order can save you up to 35% compared to doing rooms one at a time.

2

Mix Blockout & Sunscreen

Use blockout in bedrooms where you need it, and lighter sunscreen fabrics in living areas. No need for premium blockout everywhere — save where it makes sense.

3

Choose Roller Blinds

Roller blinds offer the best price-to-performance ratio. Clean, modern looks starting from just $49 per window — and they suit virtually every room.

4

Go Cordless, Skip Motorised

Cordless spring-operated blinds give you a sleek, child-safe finish at a fraction of motorised pricing. Reserve motorised only for hard-to-reach windows.

5

Neutral Colours Last Longer

White, grey, and linen tones won't date with trends. You'll save on replacements down the road and they'll match if you redecorate later.

6

Take Advantage of Seasonal Sales

We run major promotions in autumn and after EOFY. Sign up for our mailing list or check current offers to time your purchase right.
