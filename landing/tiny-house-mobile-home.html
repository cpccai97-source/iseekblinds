📐 Precision Fit
The Challenge

Standard blinds don't fit non-standard windows

Tiny houses and mobile homes come with windows that break every rule — narrower frames, unusual heights, loft angles, curved corners. Off-the-shelf blinds leave gaps, look awkward, and waste precious space. We measure to the millimetre and build each blind specifically for your home.

🪟 Space-Saving Design
Space Savers

Blinds that disappear when you don't need them

In a tiny home, every centimetre of wall and window real estate matters. Our honeycomb blinds stack to just 40mm. Our rollers tuck into compact 28mm cassettes. No dangling cords, no bulky headrails — just clean, minimal lines that let your space breathe.

🐝 Ultra-Compact Stack 🔇 Cordless Operation ⚡ Motorised Option 🧹 Easy Clean
🌡️ Temperature Control
Energy Smart

Insulation matters more when every watt counts

Small spaces heat up fast in summer and lose warmth quickly in winter. Our double-cell honeycomb blinds create an insulating air pocket that can reduce heat loss by up to 50%. For off-grid tiny homes, that's not a luxury — it's the difference between comfort and a space heater running all night.

🧊 Summer Cool 🔥 Winter Warm 💡 50% Heat Reduction 🔋 Off-Grid Friendly

Lofts, skylights & angles — we've seen it all

That triangular loft window? The skylight over your sleeping platform? The sliding barn door that needs a privacy screen? We've solved every weird window configuration a tiny house can throw at us. Specialty brackets, tension systems, magnetic catches — whatever it takes to make it work beautifully.
