🏛️ Body Corporate Compliance

Strata-Approved Blinds That Meet Every Requirement

Body corporate by-laws often mandate a uniform exterior appearance across all units. Our team works directly with your owners' corporation or building manager to ensure every installation meets the approved specifications — colour, material, and mounting method.

  • ✓
    Uniform Exterior Appearance — We supply identical street-facing blinds across all units, maintaining the complex's cohesive aesthetic as required by most strata schemes.
  • ✓
    Pre-Approved Materials & Colours — Our range includes body-corporate-friendly options in white, off-white, and neutral tones that meet standard external appearance guidelines.
  • ✓
    Documented Compliance Reports — We provide written specifications and product data sheets that can be submitted to your body corporate for pre-approval before installation.
  • ✓
    Non-Invasive Installation — Our mounting systems require no structural modifications to common property walls, frames, or facades — protecting your bond and strata compliance.

✨ Why Townhouse Owners Choose Us

Designed for Multi-Dwelling Challenges

Townhouse living presents unique challenges — shared walls, close neighbours, compact spaces, and body corporate rules. Our solutions address every one of them.

🔇

Noise Reduction Between Units

Honeycomb blinds and heavy-weight curtains create an additional sound barrier at windows — the primary noise entry point in shared-wall dwellings. Our acoustic-rated products can reduce external noise by up to 7dB, making a noticeable difference in daily comfort, especially for bedrooms facing shared driveways or courtyards.

🔒

Privacy Without Compromise

Close-set townhouses demand effective privacy solutions. Our day/night blinds, sheer-to-blockout combinations, and top-down/bottom-up honeycomb blinds let you control exactly how much the outside world sees in — without sacrificing natural light during the day. Perfect for ground-floor units and properties facing common walkways.

⚡

Energy Efficiency for Smaller Spaces

Compact townhouses heat up and cool down faster than detached homes. Well-fitted window furnishings act as a thermal barrier — keeping your space cooler in Melbourne's summers and warmer in winter. Our honeycomb blinds can reduce heating costs by up to 40% and cooling costs by up to 30%, making a real difference to your energy bills.

🎨

Interior Freedom, Exterior Uniformity

Our dual-fabric and backed-fabric systems give you the best of both worlds: choose any colour, texture, or opacity for your interior while maintaining the approved white or neutral exterior that keeps body corporate happy. One window, two personalities — compliant from the street, personalised from the inside.

📱

Smart Home & Motorisation

Townhouses often have high or hard-to-reach windows, particularly on staircases and mezzanine levels. Our motorised blinds integrate with Google Home, Apple HomeKit, and Alexa — letting you control every window with your voice or phone. Set schedules to open and close automatically, even when you're away.

👶

Child & Pet Safety

Every product we supply to multi-dwelling properties meets or exceeds Australian child safety standards. Our cordless, spring-loaded, and motorised systems eliminate dangerous dangling cords — a critical consideration for families in townhouse estates. All installations include child safety compliance certificates.

🔄 Our Process

From First Contact to Fitted Blinds

Our streamlined process is designed for multi-unit projects — minimising disruption to residents while ensuring every unit is measured, manufactured, and installed to the same exacting standard.

01

Site Assessment & Consultation

We visit your complex to assess window types, measurements, and any body corporate by-law requirements. We photograph each unit type and note any variations in window sizes or frames across the development. This initial assessment is completely free and obligation-free.

02

Sample Selection & Body Corp Submission

We prepare a curated sample board of compliant products — fabrics, colours, and materials that meet your body corporate's external appearance guidelines. We provide a written specification document suitable for committee submission, including product data sheets, colour references, and installation methodology.

03

Bulk Quote & Approval

Once products are selected, we prepare a detailed bulk quote with unit-by-unit breakdowns. Volume discounts are applied automatically. The quote includes all measuring, manufacturing, delivery, installation, and disposal of packaging. No hidden costs — the price you see is the price you pay.

04

Precision Measuring

Our technicians measure every window in every unit individually. Even in identical floor plans, window openings can vary by several millimetres — enough to cause fitting issues. We schedule measuring in blocks to minimise disruption, coordinating with property managers and tenants for access.

05

Installation & Handover

Installation is scheduled across the complex in a logical sequence — typically 3-5 units per day depending on the number of windows. Each unit is left clean and tidy, with all blinds tested and demonstrated to the resident or owner. We provide care instructions and warranty documentation for every unit individually.
