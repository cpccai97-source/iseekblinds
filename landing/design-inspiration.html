From the Experts

Styling Tips

Professional advice to elevate your window treatments.

01

Mount Ceiling-High

Install curtain tracks or blinds as close to the ceiling as possible. It draws the eye upward and makes rooms feel taller and more luxurious.

02

Layer for Depth

Combine two treatments — sheers with rollers, curtains with shutters. Layering adds richness and gives you flexible light control options.

03

Match the Architecture

Modern homes suit clean roller blinds; heritage homes shine with plantation shutters. Let the building's character guide your choices.

04

Consider the Light

North-facing rooms need UV protection; south-facing rooms benefit from light-maximising sheers. Always design with your orientation in mind.
