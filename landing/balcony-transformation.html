Urban Garden

Your plants called. They want better blinds.

Balcony gardens need light management. Too much direct sun scorches your herbs; too little and your ferns sulk. Our adjustable outdoor blinds let you dial in the perfect light — filtered morning sun, full shade at noon, open sky at dusk.

  • Adjustable openness to control light for different plants
  • Wind protection for delicate plants and vertical gardens
  • Durable fabrics that resist mould and moisture
  • Half-drop positioning for partial coverage flexibility
Protect Your Garden

  • Anti-glare fabrics designed for screen-visible environments
  • Reduces temperature by up to 8°C on hot days
  • Easy one-hand operation for quick adjustments between meetings
