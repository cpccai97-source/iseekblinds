How Day Night Blinds Work

A clever dual-layer fabric system that rotates to align sheer and opaque bands, giving you seamless light control.

Sheer Panel Opaque Panel

One Blind. Infinite Possibilities.

Day night blinds (also called zebra or vision blinds) feature two layers of fabric with alternating sheer and opaque bands. By adjusting the chain or motor, the bands align differently:

  • 1 Open position — Sheer bands align for soft, filtered natural light while maintaining street-level privacy
  • 2 Closed position — Opaque bands overlap for near-complete blockout, perfect for bedrooms and media rooms
  • 3 Anywhere in between — Micro-adjust to find the perfect balance for any time of day

Day Mode vs Night Mode

Two personalities in one elegant window treatment

☀️ Day Mode

Sheer bands aligned

  • 💡 Soft, diffused natural light
  • 👀 See out, but others can't see in
  • 🌡️ Reduces glare while keeping warmth
  • 🎨 Beautiful striped light patterns
  • 🍃 Garden views maintained

🌙 Night Mode

Opaque bands overlapped

  • 🌑 Near-complete light blockout
  • 🔒 Total privacy from outside
  • ❄️ Extra insulation layer
  • 😴 Ideal for bedrooms & shift workers
  • 🎬 Perfect for media rooms
