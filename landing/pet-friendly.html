The Struggle Is Real

Common Pet vs. Blinds Problems 🐱

Every pet owner knows the pain. Here's what we hear from Melbourne pet parents every day.

😼

Cats Climbing Blinds

Cats love vertical blinds like a personal jungle gym. Bent slats, broken chains, and dangling cords become their favourite toys.

🐕

Dogs Nosing Through

Dogs push their noses through blinds to watch the world go by, bending slats and leaving nose prints on every surface.

🦷

Chewing & Scratching

Puppies and anxious dogs chew blind cords and bottom rails. Cats scratch fabric blinds when they can't get to the window.

⚠️

Cord Dangers

Dangling cords are a serious strangulation risk for curious pets. Cordless operation isn't just convenient — it's essential.

🧹

Fur Everywhere

Pet hair clings to fabric blinds and curtains. Standard materials are fur magnets that need constant cleaning.

☀️

UV Damage to Furniture

Pets love sunny spots — but UV rays fade your floors, couches, and pet beds. Protection matters for furniture longevity.

Our Solutions

Pet-Proof Window Treatments

Designed to handle everything your pets throw at them — literally.

🪟

Plantation Shutters

The ultimate pet-proof option. Solid timber or PVC that cats can't climb and dogs can't chew through.

  • Virtually indestructible PVC option
  • No cords, no chains, no dangling parts
  • Adjustable louvres for pet viewing
  • Easy wipe-clean surfaces
  • Won't collect pet hair
🎞️

Roller Blinds

When retracted, they're completely out of reach. Choose scratch-resistant fabrics for added durability.

  • Rolls up completely out of pet reach
  • Cordless spring or motorised operation
  • PVC-coated fabrics resist scratching
  • Easy to replace fabric if damaged
  • Anti-static options repel pet hair
🏠

Honeycomb Blinds

Lightweight, cordless, and surprisingly tough. The cellular structure adds insulation for comfortable pet napping spots.

  • Cordless top-down/bottom-up
  • Lets pets see out while blocking UV
  • Anti-static fabric resists fur
  • Excellent insulation for pet comfort
  • Motorised option keeps them safe
Material Guide

Pet Durability Ratings

Not all materials are created equal. Here's how they stack up against your fur family.

🪨

PVC / Faux Wood

Waterproof, scratch-resistant, and nearly indestructible. The best choice for destructive pets.

Durability: ★★★★★
🪵

Solid Timber

Beautiful and tough. Minor scratches add character. Easily refinished if needed.

Durability: ★★★★☆
🧵

PVC-Coated Fabric

Wipe-clean surface resists stains and light scratching. Great for roller blinds.

Durability: ★★★★☆
🫧

Anti-Static Polyester

Repels pet hair and dust. Easy vacuum cleaning. Moderate scratch resistance.

Durability: ★★★☆☆
Safety First

Keeping Pets Safe at Home

Every product we recommend is assessed for pet safety as standard.

  • 🚫
