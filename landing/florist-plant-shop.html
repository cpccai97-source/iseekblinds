Your Botanical Blinds Journey

From first call to flourishing shop — here's how we bring the perfect light to your space.

🌱
Step 1

We Visit Your Space

Our specialist walks through your shop, noting window orientation, sun exposure patterns, and which displays get hit hardest. We map your light — morning to close.

✨ We even check which blooms sit where
🌸
Step 2

Custom Light Plan

Not every window needs the same treatment. Your storefront might want sheer elegance, while the back workroom needs full control. We design a zone-by-zone plan that matches how your shop actually lives.

🎨 Colour-matched to your brand aesthetic
☀️
Step 3

Fabric & Material Selection

Touch, feel, hold against the light. We bring samples to your actual windows — because what looks great in a showroom might clash with your eucalyptus wall. Humidity-resistant, easy-clean options for spaces that get misty.

🧪 Moisture & mildew resistant options
🌍
Step 4

Precision Install

We work around your schedule — early mornings or after close. No disruption to your trading day. Installed, tested, adjusted until the light falls exactly where your flowers want it.

⏰ Before-hours install available
🌿
Step 5

Watch Things Bloom

Longer-lasting displays. Happier succulents. Customers who linger because the space just feels right. We check back in a month to make sure everything's still perfect.

🌻 Flowers last 2–3 days longer on average

Picks for Botanical Spaces

Sunscreen Roller Blinds

The workhorse of florist shops. Filters harsh UV while keeping your storefront visible from outside. Available in 1–5% openness — enough to protect roses without hiding them.

UV filtering See-through Easy clean

Sheer Curtains

Soft, diffused light that makes every petal glow. Perfect for creating that dreamy, Instagram-worthy atmosphere your customers love photographing. Light enough to dance with the air conditioning.

Soft light Elegant Photo-friendly

Timber-Look Venetians

Adjustable slats let you dial in exactly how much light each zone gets — tilt open for the ferns, half-closed over the orchids. The timber aesthetic complements any botanical interior.

Motorised Smart Blinds

Program them to follow the sun. Morning: wide open for eastern light. Afternoon: filtered to protect west-facing displays. All hands-free — because yours are busy arranging bouquets.
