Why Go Smart?

Transform your windows from static coverings into intelligent, responsive systems

🎙️

Voice Control

"Hey Google, close the living room blinds." Hands-free control through any voice assistant. Set up rooms, groups, and scenes for multi-blind control with a single command.

📱

App Control

Control every blind from anywhere in the world through your smartphone. Set precise positions, create schedules, and monitor status — even when you're on holiday.

⏰

Smart Scheduling

Set blinds to open with sunrise and close at sunset — automatically adjusted by season. Create wake-up routines, movie-time scenes, and bedtime schedules.

🌡️

Climate Response

Pair with temperature sensors to automatically close blinds when rooms get too hot. Save on cooling costs while maintaining comfort throughout the day.

🔒

Security Simulation

When you're away, smart blinds can simulate occupancy by opening and closing at random intervals. Integrates with security systems for a comprehensive smart home setup.

👶

Child Safe by Design

Motorised blinds are inherently child-safe — no cords, no chains, no hazards. The safest option for families with young children, meeting all Australian standards.

Deep Integration

Not just basic control — full ecosystem integration with your favourite smart home platforms

🟢
Google Home

Google Home & Nest

Full integration with Google Home ecosystem. Use voice commands through any Google Nest speaker, Hub, or your Android phone. Create routines that combine blinds with lights, heating, and more.

  • Voice commands: "Hey Google, set bedroom blinds to 50%"
  • Google Home routines — blinds as part of Good Morning/Goodnight
  • Room assignment for grouped control
  • Works with Nest thermostats for climate-responsive automation
🔵
Amazon Alexa

Amazon Alexa

Seamless Alexa integration through Echo devices and the Alexa app. Create custom skills, group blinds by room, and build complex automations with Alexa Routines.

  • Natural language: "Alexa, open all the blinds"
  • Alexa Routines with time, motion, and sensor triggers
  • Multi-room audio + blinds scenes for movie nights
  • Compatible with Ring, Blink, and other Amazon smart home devices
⚪
Apple HomeKit

Apple HomeKit

Native HomeKit support means your blinds appear in the Apple Home app alongside all your other devices. Use Siri voice control, create automations, and include blinds in HomeKit scenes.

  • Siri control: "Hey Siri, close the curtains"
  • Apple Home automations with time-of-day and sensor triggers
  • HomeKit Secure — encrypted, privacy-first control
  • Control from iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac, and HomePod

Installation Process

From consultation to fully automated blinds in 5 simple steps

01

Smart Home Assessment

We assess your existing smart home setup, Wi-Fi coverage, and window types. We'll recommend the best motor and control system for your ecosystem.

02

Product Selection

Choose from our range of smart-compatible blinds, curtains, and shutters. Browse fabrics, colours, and motor options. We'll provide samples to take home.

03

Precision Measurement

Our technicians measure every window with laser precision. We also map power outlets and Wi-Fi signal strength for optimal motor placement.

04

Professional Installation

Expert fitting with clean cable management. Battery motors need no electrician. Hardwired options available for new builds.

05

Smart Setup & Training
