The Challenge

Melbourne's hospitality scene is one of the most competitive in Australia. From laneway cafes in the CBD to waterfront restaurants in St Kilda, every detail of the dining experience matters — including your windows.

Glare ruining the mood at brunch? Outdoor diners baking in the afternoon sun? A street-facing window that offers no privacy for intimate dinners? The right blinds solve all of these problems while adding to your venue's character.

Our Approach

We've fitted out dozens of Melbourne restaurants and cafes with window solutions tailored to the hospitality industry. We understand that your needs are different from residential — you need durability, easy cleaning, fire safety compliance, and fast turnaround to minimise trading downtime.

"The right window treatment turns a dining space from functional to unforgettable."

Atmosphere

Master Your Venue's Ambiance

Light is the single most important factor in dining ambiance after food and service. Too bright feels like a canteen. Too dark and guests can't read the menu. Window treatments give you precise control.

Our motorised systems can be programmed to shift throughout the day — open and airy for morning coffee, filtered for lunch service, dimmed for evening dining — all automatically.

  • Morning: sheers for soft, welcoming light
  • Midday: sunscreen blinds to cut western glare
  • Evening: translucent or blockout for intimacy
  • Events: one-touch presets for functions
  • Seasons: adjust for Melbourne's changing light

Laneway Cafes

Small frontages, heritage buildings, narrow spaces. We specialise in custom solutions for Melbourne's iconic laneways — from Degraves Street to Hardware Lane. Our blinds work within heritage overlays and council requirements.

Outdoor Dining

Melbourne's alfresco culture demands all-weather solutions. Our outdoor blinds let you trade through winter rain and summer heat. Wind-rated for the notorious Melbourne gusts, with clear PVC options to maintain those views.

Waterfront Venues

Docklands, St Kilda, Williamstown — waterfront venues cop salt, wind and harsh reflection off the water. We use marine-grade components and UV-stabilised fabrics built to handle the conditions without compromising on style.

Rooftop Bars

Retractable blinds and screens for rooftop bars that need to work in sun, wind and rain. Our motorised systems let staff adapt to Melbourne's "four seasons in one day" weather quickly and easily.

Fine Dining
