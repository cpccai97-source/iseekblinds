The Container Problem

Why Off-the-Shelf
Doesn't Work

Container homes break every convention window furnishings were designed for. Here's what we solve.

01

Steel Frame Mounting

Standard brackets assume timber. Container walls are corrugated steel — 1.6mm Corten that laughs at self-tapping screws. Every mounting point needs to be pre-drilled with hardened steel fixings.

Our solution Custom magnetic and clamp-mount bracket systems designed for corrugated steel profiles. No drilling required for most installations.
02

Non-Standard Openings

Container windows are cut to order. No two are alike. Standard sizes don't exist in this world — every blind must be made to measure, down to the millimetre.

Our solution Precision digital measurement with laser templating for any opening shape or size.
03

Condensation & Heat

Steel walls create thermal bridging nightmares. Condensation, extreme heat gain, and cold spots make fabric selection critical — the wrong material won't survive a Melbourne winter.

Our solution Moisture-resistant, mould-proof fabrics with thermal-backed options to combat condensation and regulate temperature.
