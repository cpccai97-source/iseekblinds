The Humidity Problem Nobody Warns You About

Your indoor pool maintains 50–70% relative humidity. Condensation coats every surface. Standard blinds? They don't stand a chance.

  • Fabric blinds grow mould within weeks
  • Timber warps and delaminates at the joints
  • Metal components corrode and seize up
  • Standard coatings peel and bubble from moisture
  • Adhesives fail in constant warm-humid air
