The Challenge

Dance studios have unique lighting demands that standard blinds can't meet

🪞

Mirror Glare

Floor-to-ceiling mirrors amplify sunlight into blinding reflections. Dancers need controlled, even light to see their form — not squint through sun-streaked glass.

🩰

Privacy for Changing

Studios often double as changing areas. Street-facing windows need instant privacy without permanently blocking the natural light dancers love for daytime classes.

🌡️

Heat & Comfort

Dancers generate serious heat. West-facing studios can become unbearable by afternoon. Effective solar blinds reduce temperature while maintaining the studio's airy feel.

Our Process

Choreographed like a perfect routine

STEP ONE

Studio Assessment

We visit during class hours to understand how light moves through your space. Window orientation, mirror placement, and peak sun times all factor in.

STEP TWO

Product Recommendation

Based on your studio's unique needs, we recommend specific fabrics, opacity levels, and control systems. Samples provided for you to see in your own light.

STEP THREE

Precise Measurement

Millimetre-accurate measurements ensure perfect fit. We account for brackets, stacking space, and clearance from barre rails and speakers.

STEP FOUR

Silent Installation

Installed outside class times with zero disruption. We clean up completely — your studio is ready for the next session.
