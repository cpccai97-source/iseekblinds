The Coastal Challenge

Living near the ocean is beautiful — but your window treatments face harsh conditions that standard products can't handle.

🧂

Salt Spray Corrosion

Airborne salt attacks metal components, causing rust and deterioration. Standard steel brackets and aluminium can corrode within months in exposed coastal positions.

☀️

Intense UV Exposure

Coastal homes cop full sun reflected off water and sand. UV degrades fabrics faster, fades colours and brittles plastics. You need materials rated for extreme UV.

💧

Humidity & Moisture

Sea breezes carry constant moisture. Timber warps, fabrics grow mould, and mechanisms seize up. Coastal window treatments must be inherently moisture-resistant.

Recommended Products

These products are specifically chosen for their performance in coastal environments

Salt Resistant

Aluminium Venetian Blinds

Marine-grade aluminium slats resist salt corrosion and wipe clean easily. Excellent light control with a clean, contemporary look.

Moisture Proof

PVC Plantation Shutters

Unlike timber, PVC shutters won't warp, swell or crack from moisture. They look identical to wood but are impervious to coastal conditions.

  • 100% waterproof — perfect for bathrooms too

  • Classic Hamptons aesthetic

Coastal Styling Tips

Create that relaxed beachside feel with the right window treatments

Hamptons White

Crisp white PVC shutters with wide louvres are the quintessential coastal look. Pair with natural timber floors and linen furnishings for that breezy Hamptons feel.

Natural Textures

Layer sheer curtains over roller blinds in sandy or oatmeal tones. The sheers soften the light while the roller provides UV protection — functional and beautiful.

Modern Coastal

Charcoal or navy sunscreen rollers frame ocean views without the glare. Pair with matte black hardware for a contemporary coastal aesthetic.
