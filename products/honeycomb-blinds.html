How To Measure

Measuring Guide

📏 Inside Mount

Measure width at top, middle and bottom — use the narrowest. Measure drop from top of recess to sill. Minimum recess depth: 40mm.

📐 Outside Mount

Measure the area to cover, adding 40mm each side for light blocking. Measure from bracket position to below sill.
