Honeycomb Blinds

The most energy efficient window covering. Save on heating and cooling while looking great.

Save Energy, Save Money

Honeycomb blinds (also called cellular blinds) are engineered with a unique cellular structure that traps air, creating a natural insulation barrier at your windows. This means less heat escaping in winter and less heat entering in summer.

Studies show that honeycomb blinds can reduce heat loss through windows by up to 40%, significantly lowering your energy bills. They're also whisper-quiet and beautifully sleek — a smart choice in every sense.

✅Up to 40% energy savings
✅Single & double cell options
✅Blockout & light filtering
✅Cordless & motorised options

How Honeycomb Blinds Save Energy

❄️

Winter Warmth

The cellular pockets trap warm air and prevent heat from escaping through your windows. Keep your heater working less and your home cosier.

Up to 40% less heat loss
☀️

Summer Cool

Block radiant heat from entering through sun-exposed windows. Reduce the load on your air conditioning and stay comfortable naturally.

Up to 30% less heat gain
💰

Lower Bills

By reducing the energy needed to heat and cool your home, honeycomb blinds can save hundreds of dollars per year on energy bills.

Save $200-500/year

Cell Options

Choose the right cellular structure for your needs

⬡

Single Cell

One layer of honeycomb cells provides excellent insulation and a clean, streamlined look. Perfect for most rooms in your home. Our most popular option.

  • Great insulation performance
  • Slimmer profile
  • Lighter weight
  • More affordable
  • Suits most applications
⬡⬡

Double Cell

Two layers of honeycomb cells stacked together for maximum insulation. The premium choice for south-facing windows, large glass areas and homes prioritising energy efficiency.

  • Maximum insulation
  • Superior energy efficiency
  • Better noise reduction
  • Ideal for large windows
  • Premium performance
