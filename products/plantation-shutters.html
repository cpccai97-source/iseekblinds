How To Measure

Shutter Measuring Guide

📏 Window Width

Measure the width at the top, middle and bottom of the window recess. Use the narrowest measurement. Shutters are precision-fit.

📐 Window Height

Measure the height at the left, centre and right of the recess. Use the tallest measurement for shutters.
