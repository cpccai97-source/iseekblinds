Plantation Shutters

The ultimate in style, durability and home value. A lifetime investment for your windows.

Add Value & Style to Your Home

Plantation shutters are more than just a window covering — they're a home improvement. Known for their clean lines, adjustable louvres and lasting durability, shutters add a premium feel to any room while increasing your property value.

Our shutters are custom-made to fit any window shape, including arches, circles, bay windows and sliding doors. Choose from PVC, Basswood or Aluminium to suit your style and budget.

✅Increases property value
✅Custom made for any window shape
✅25+ year lifespan
✅Virtually maintenance free

Material Options

Three premium materials to choose from

PVC Shutters

Our most popular and cost-effective option. PVC shutters are waterproof, durable and perfect for wet areas like bathrooms, kitchens and laundries. They won't warp, crack or fade.

  • 100% waterproof
  • Won't warp or crack
  • Most affordable option
  • Easy to clean
  • UV resistant

Basswood Shutters

Beautiful real timber shutters with a natural grain finish. Basswood is lightweight yet strong, and can be painted or stained to match any interior style. The premium choice for living areas and bedrooms.

  • Real timber beauty
  • Lightweight & strong
  • Paint or stain finish
  • Natural wood grain
  • Premium look & feel

Aluminium Shutters

The ultimate in strength and weather resistance. Aluminium shutters are ideal for outdoor areas, balconies and large openings. They handle extreme weather and won't rust or corrode.

  • Indoor & outdoor use
  • Extreme weather resistant
  • Won't rust or corrode
  • Large span capability
  • Powder-coated finish
