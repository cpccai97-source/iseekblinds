Curtains

Luxurious custom curtains that add warmth, elegance and character to any room.

Timeless Elegance, Modern Style

Nothing transforms a room quite like beautiful curtains. They add softness, texture and a sense of luxury that other window coverings simply can't match. At iSeekBlinds, we custom-make curtains in a wide range of styles, fabrics and colours to suit any interior.

Curtain Styles

Choose from our three most popular heading styles

S-Fold (Ripple Fold)

The most popular curtain style in modern Australian homes. S-Fold curtains create soft, continuous waves that glide effortlessly along the track. Clean lines and contemporary elegance.

  • Soft, flowing wave effect
  • Modern & contemporary look
  • Smooth opening & closing
  • Works with all fabric types
  • Perfect for open-plan living

Pinch Pleat

The classic curtain style that never goes out of fashion. Pinch pleat curtains feature fabric gathered into neat, crisp pleats — available in single, double or triple pleat designs.

  • Timeless, classic look
  • Single, double or triple pleat
  • Formal & elegant appearance
  • Great fabric drape
  • Ideal for traditional interiors

Sheer Curtains

Delicate, translucent curtains that let light flow through while maintaining a sense of privacy. Often paired with blockout curtains or roller blinds for a layered look.

  • Beautiful diffused light
  • Daytime privacy
  • Elegant, airy feel
  • Layer with blockout for versatility
  • Available in S-Fold or Pleat
