Venetian Blinds

Classic horizontal slats for precise light control. A timeless choice for any interior.

Precise Light Control at Your Fingertips

Venetian blinds are the original adjustable window covering, and they remain one of the most practical and stylish options available. With horizontal slats that tilt to any angle, you get complete control over light, airflow and privacy.

Our venetian blinds come in three material options — real timber for warmth and character, aluminium for modern sleekness, and faux wood for the best of both worlds. All custom-made to your exact measurements.

✅Precise light & privacy control
✅50mm or 63mm slat widths
✅Wide colour range
✅Cord or wand operation

Material Options

Choose the material that best suits your space

Timber Venetians

Real timber venetian blinds bring natural warmth and character to your space. Available in a range of stains and painted finishes, timber venetians suit both modern and traditional interiors.

  • Natural wood grain beauty
  • Stain or paint finish
  • 50mm or 63mm slats
  • Warm, rich aesthetic
  • Eco-friendly materials

Aluminium Venetians

Sleek, lightweight and modern. Aluminium venetians are perfect for contemporary spaces, offices and wet areas. Available in 25mm or 50mm slats with a huge colour range.

  • 25mm or 50mm slats
  • Moisture resistant
  • Lightweight & durable
  • Huge colour range
  • Budget friendly

Faux Wood Venetians

The look of real timber with the durability of PVC. Faux wood venetians are moisture-resistant, easy to clean and won't warp or fade. Great for bathrooms, kitchens and high-humidity areas.

  • Looks like real timber
  • 100% moisture proof
  • Won't warp or fade
  • Easy to clean
  • Great value option
