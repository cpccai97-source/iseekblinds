⚡ Motorised Solutions

Smart blinds and curtains that work with your lifestyle. Control with your phone, remote or voice.

Your Home, Smarter

Motorised window coverings are no longer a luxury — they're an accessible upgrade that transforms how you interact with your home. Imagine waking up to blinds that open automatically at sunrise, or closing all blinds at once with a single voice command.

We offer motorisation solutions for virtually all of our products — roller blinds, curtains, honeycomb blinds and more. Whether you want a simple remote control or full smart home integration, we have the right solution for you.

Control Options

Choose the control method that suits your lifestyle

🎮

Remote Control

Simple handheld remote to open, close and stop your blinds. Ideal for single rooms or anyone who prefers a straightforward solution.

📱

Smartphone App

Control your blinds from anywhere via your phone. Perfect for when you're away from home or in bed and don't want to get up.

🗣️

Voice Control

Compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Home and Apple HomeKit. Just say "Hey Google, close the blinds" and it's done.

⏰

Scheduling

Set automated schedules — open blinds at sunrise, close them at sunset, or create custom routines tied to your daily schedule.

🏠

Smart Home Scenes

Create "scenes" like Movie Mode, Morning Routine or Away Mode that control multiple blinds simultaneously.

🌤️

Sun Sensors

Optional sun sensors automatically close blinds when direct sunlight hits, protecting your furniture and keeping rooms cool.
