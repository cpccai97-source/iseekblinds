How To Measure

Measuring Guide

📏 Inside Mount

Measure the width at the top, middle and bottom of the window recess. Use the narrowest measurement. Measure the drop from the top of the recess to the sill.

📐 Outside Mount

Measure the width you want to cover. We recommend adding 50mm each side for better light blocking. Measure the drop from bracket position to below the sill.

💡 Free Measure

Not sure? Our team will come out and measure for free — no obligation. Always measure in millimetres for accuracy.
