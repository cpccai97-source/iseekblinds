Roller Blinds

Sleek, modern and versatile. The perfect window covering for any room in your home.

Australia's Most Popular Window Covering

Roller blinds are the go-to choice for Australian homeowners, and for good reason. They're clean, simple and incredibly functional. Whether you need complete darkness for a bedroom, UV protection for a living area, or soft filtered light for a study, roller blinds deliver.

Choose Your Fabric Type

Three options to suit every room and lifestyle

Blockout

Complete light blocking for bedrooms, home theatres and nurseries. Our blockout roller blinds provide 100% darkness when fully closed, helping you sleep better and keeping rooms cool.

  • 100% light blocking
  • Maximum privacy
  • Energy efficient
  • Ideal for bedrooms

Sunscreen

See-through fabric that blocks UV rays while maintaining your view. Sunscreen blinds reduce glare and protect furniture from sun damage while letting natural light flood in.

  • UV protection up to 99%
  • Maintains outward view
  • Reduces glare
  • Ideal for living areas

Light Filtering

Softly diffuse light while providing privacy during the day. Light filtering blinds create a warm, gentle ambience perfect for living rooms, dining areas and kitchens.

  • Soft, diffused light
  • Daytime privacy
  • Wide colour range
  • Ideal for kitchens & living rooms

How to Choose the Right Roller Blind

1

Consider the Room

Bedrooms need blockout, living areas suit sunscreen, and kitchens work well with light filtering or moisture-resistant fabrics.

2

Choose Inside or Outside Mount

Inside mount sits within the window frame for a sleek look. Outside mount covers the full window area for better light blocking.

3

Pick Your Colour & Fabric

Consider your room's colour scheme, furniture and the amount of light you want. We offer free fabric samples.

4

Add Motorisation (Optional)

Make life easier with motorised roller blinds. Control via remote, phone app, or voice assistants like Alexa and Google.

Measuring Guide

Not sure how to measure? We offer free professional measuring, or use this guide for a rough idea.

📏 Inside Mount

Measure the width at the top, middle and bottom of the window recess. Use the narrowest measurement. Measure the drop from the top of the recess to the window sill.

📐 Outside Mount

Measure the width of the area you want to cover. We recommend adding 50mm each side for better light blocking. Measure the drop from where the bracket will sit to below the sill.

💡 Pro Tip

Always measure in millimetres for accuracy. If you're unsure, our team will come out and measure for free — no obligation whatsoever.
