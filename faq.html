Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

🪟 Products

We offer a comprehensive range including roller blinds (blockout, sunscreen, light-filtering), curtains (S-fold, pinch pleat, sheer), plantation shutters, venetian blinds (timber, aluminium, faux wood), honeycomb blinds, and motorised solutions. All products are custom-made to fit your windows perfectly.

Yes, the majority of our products are manufactured right here in Australia. We work with trusted local suppliers to ensure quality materials and craftsmanship. Some specialty products may be sourced internationally but always meet our strict quality standards.

Blockout roller blinds completely block all light, making them ideal for bedrooms and media rooms. Sunscreen blinds filter light while reducing glare and UV rays, allowing you to see outside during the day — perfect for living areas and offices. We also offer light-filtering options that sit between the two.

Absolutely! Our motorised blinds can be controlled via remote control, smartphone app, or voice assistants like Google Home and Alexa. They run on rechargeable batteries or can be hardwired. Motorisation is available for roller blinds, curtains and honeycomb blinds. It's a great option for hard-to-reach windows or smart home integration.

📏 Measuring

No — we provide a free professional measure service. Our experienced consultant will visit your home, take precise measurements, and discuss the best options for each window. This ensures a perfect fit every time. However, if you'd like a rough estimate first, you're welcome to provide approximate measurements.

Inside mount (recess fit) means the blind sits inside the window frame for a clean, streamlined look. Outside mount (face fit) means the blind sits on the wall above and around the window, providing better light blockage and making windows appear larger. Our consultant will recommend the best option based on your window type.

Yes, 100% free with absolutely no obligation. Our consultant will visit your home at a time that suits you, measure your windows, show you fabric and colour samples, and provide a detailed quote. There's no pressure to commit — take your time to decide.

🔧 Installation

Installation time varies depending on the number of windows and product type. As a guide, a standard home with 5–10 windows typically takes 2–4 hours. Plantation shutters may take a little longer due to their complexity. Our installers work efficiently and leave your home clean and tidy.

Yes, we require someone over 18 to be present during installation. This allows our installer to walk you through the operation and care of your new window furnishings, and to ensure you're happy with the final result before signing off.

Most products are manufactured and installed within 2–3 weeks from order confirmation. Some specialty products or custom colours may take 3–4 weeks. We'll provide a clear timeline at the time of order and keep you updated throughout the process.

🛡️ Warranty

All our products come with a comprehensive 5-year warranty covering manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship. This includes the fabric, components, and mechanisms. Motorised products also include warranty on the motor and electronics.

Our warranty covers defects in materials, mechanisms (chains, springs, motors), and fabric (excluding normal wear and tear, fading from direct sun exposure, or damage caused by misuse). If a component fails under normal use within the warranty period, we'll repair or replace it free of charge.

Simply contact us by phone or email with your order number and a description of the issue. We'll arrange a time to inspect and assess the claim, usually within a few business days. If the issue is covered under warranty, we'll organise the repair or replacement at no cost.

💰 Pricing

Pricing depends on the product type, window size, and fabric selection. As a rough guide: roller blinds start from around $150 per window, venetian blinds from $180, curtains from $250, and plantation shutters from $350. The best way to get accurate pricing is to book a free measure & quote.
