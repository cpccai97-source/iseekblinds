About iSeekBlinds

Melbourne's trusted window furnishing specialists

Our Story

Founded in Melbourne, iSeekBlinds began with a simple mission: to make premium window furnishings accessible to every Australian home and business. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Melbourne's most trusted window furnishing companies.

Over the past decade, we've helped more than 1,000 homeowners and businesses transform their spaces with custom-made blinds, curtains and shutters. Our success is built on three pillars: quality products, honest pricing and exceptional customer service.

We work directly with Australian manufacturers to ensure every product meets our exacting standards. From the initial consultation to final installation, our experienced team handles everything — so you can sit back and enjoy the results.
